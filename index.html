About Us

Assistance To Independence

At Assistance To Independence Home Care Services, our mission is to work together as a team to provide individuals the opportunity to remain living in their own homes, while assisting them in achieving their goals. We provide the highest quality personal care for individuals that need assistance with Meal prep, meal planning, community inclusion, medication assistance, bathing, feeding, money management and transportation.

To support our vision, Assistance To Independence Home Care Services provides highly dedicated and reliable caregivers. We respect the individuality, dignity, and diversity of those that seek assistance and we treat them with utmost dignity, respect, and compassion.

Our goal is to assist individuals to achieve their maximum potential for independence in living and obtaining an optimal quality of life. At Assistance To Independence we strive to make the individuals home care a pleasant experience. We consider you part of our family.
